My Life Philosophy





This is a story of when I took an organ (instrument) class only because it didn't have any prerequisites.

Needless to say, I failed at it and felt very inadequate.

One day I saw that someone had put this small sticky note on the organ I chose.

I felt like hope was restored to my life again.

This defining moment taught me that sometimes I bite off more than I can chew.

At times, that is okay. All I have to do is to remember one of my favorite quotes:

"Come What May and Love it!" ~ Joseph B. Wirthlin
